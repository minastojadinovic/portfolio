Digital age has reshaped how we experience grief, making it a shared experience rather than an isolated one, amplified by the power of technology and social media. Through a series of videos, I delve into the darker facets of loss, depicting the stages of decadence, disintegration, isolation, and despair. The videos’ eerie and unsettling atmosphere mirrors the cyclical nature of the grieving process, conveying its ups and downs. Amidst the pain and suffering, we catch glimpses of beauty and resilience, which reminds us that even in the darkest moments, there is always the promise of bright blue skies ahead.
