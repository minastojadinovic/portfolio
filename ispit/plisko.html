Space toys

The Pliško family are hybrid stuffed toys that serve as substitutes for all beloved living beings on an eternal journey into space and everyone creates their own toy. Pliško can be a reflection of your emotions about traveling to an unknown space, as well as what emotions you feel about leaving the known world; it is allowed for the toy to remind you of a familiar life that you are leaving behind. The idea is to see through these toys how everyone experiences a journey into the unknown, with no possibility of return, and what kind of creatures each of us would like to have next to us. Do stuffed toys necessarily have to be nice and pleasant or can they be unsettling, uncomfortable and undefined. What kind of being would each of us want on such a journey?
